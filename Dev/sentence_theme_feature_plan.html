Language Duel planning board

Sentence Themes: mixed-content duels without making the app feel split in two

This board turns the interview into an implementation artifact. It assumes sentence sets are real themes, sentence rounds can mix with word questions in the same duel deck, and word-theme behavior should be copied wherever there is no sentence-specific reason to differ.

2 theme content types: word and sentence
3 distractor words per sentence round
30s PvP, Relay, and self-duel sentence timer
45s PvE shared sentence timer
Operating principle Copy word-theme behavior unless a sentence rule explicitly overrides it. That covers sharing, archive, delete, duplicate, generate more, Pick & Prune, repair validation, theme cards, and most permissions.

North Star

Sentence themes should feel like a natural extension of themes, not a second product. The unique part is the play surface: users build Spanish sentences from word tiles.

v1 scope
Content

Sentence set = Theme

A sentence set is a new theme content type. It lives in Themes, uses the same ownership/sharing model, and appears in theme selectors.

Session

Mixed decks are allowed

A duel deck can contain word questions and sentence rounds together. Items are shuffled into one deck.

Modes

PvP, PvE, Relay, self-duel

All duel-style modes support sentence rounds. Normal solo practice does not support sentence themes in v1.

Locked Decisions

These are the answers from the interview, grouped so they can be challenged later without rereading the transcript.

decision log

Theme behavior

  • Sentence themes are sentence-only. Word themes stay word-only.
  • The existing Themes screen gets filters/tabs for word vs sentence themes.
  • Theme list cards need a visible sentence badge/icon and round count.
  • Sharing, friend editing, archive, delete, duplicate, manual add, generate more, and Pick & Prune copy word-theme behavior.
  • No TTS for sentence themes in v1. The "TTS up to date" pill simply does not render on sentence theme cards (no reserved placeholder) — TTS support will be added later.

Sentence round shape

  • English prompt -> build Spanish sentence.
  • Store the editable source: English prompt, Spanish sentence text, and 3 distractor words.
  • Gameplay splits Spanish sentence on spaces. Punctuation stays attached to words.
  • Correct sentence length is 2 to 8 words.
  • Distractors are single words only.
  • Sentence rounds store exactly 3 distractors. Word themes store 6 wrong answers (subset shown per difficulty in lib/difficultyUtils.ts). This asymmetry is intentional — the tile board already adds cognitive load.
  • Spanish commas, semicolons, quotes, parentheses, and slashes are forbidden in v1.

Validation

  • Use the same normalization spirit as word themes: case/accent/spacing-normalized comparisons for duplicates and conflicts.
  • Duplicate sentence rounds are invalid after normalization.
  • Correct words may repeat inside a sentence.
  • Distractors must be unique after normalization.
  • Distractors must not match any correct word after normalization.
  • If a correct word repeats, any identical available tile can satisfy the next matching slot. Matching is text-equivalence (after normalization), not tile-index identity — tiles with the same text are interchangeable.

Generation

  • Direct sentence-theme generation in v1.
  • User chooses 5 to 15 desired rounds, default 10.
  • Pick & Prune over-generates by 100% (default 10 → 20), matching PICK_AND_PRUNE_WORD_COUNT for word themes.
  • Pick & Prune can keep any number as long as at least 1 round remains.
  • Generator adapts to prompt skill/grammar, with beginner/lower-intermediate as safe default.
  • No grammar notes in v1.

Mixed session behavior

  • Word and sentence items are shuffled together.
  • Round identity comes from the play surface itself (tile board vs option chips). No "Sentence" type pill in the duel header — word rounds don't show one, sentence rounds don't either.
  • Difficulty pill ("EASY (+1)", "MEDIUM (+1.5)", "HARD (+2)") only renders on word rounds. That slot is empty on sentence rounds.
  • Word-only tools simply do not mount on sentence rounds. No "disabled" or "no hints" labels — just absence.
  • PvE hints do not apply to sentence rounds in v1.
  • PvP sabotages do not apply to sentence rounds in v1.
  • Relay hard-upgrade tokens stay word-only in v1.

Timers and feedback

  • PvP sentence timer: 30 seconds.
  • Relay sentence timer: 30 seconds.
  • Self-duel sentence timer: 30 seconds.
  • PvE sentence timer: 45 seconds shared team timer.
  • Sentence round feedback always reveals the correct Spanish sentence.
  • Main play prompt is the English prompt only.

Generation And Editing

Users should be able to create, review, prune, edit, manually add, and generate more sentence rounds. The user-facing flow should feel like the current word-theme flow with sentence-specific fields.

authoring

New sentence theme

  • User selects Sentence theme in New Theme.
  • Inputs: name, description, prompt/details, desired count.
  • AI returns about 150% of requested rounds for Pick & Prune.
  • User removes weak rounds and saves kept rounds.

Sentence editor

  • English prompt as text field.
  • Spanish sentence as one text field.
  • Three distractor word fields.
  • Repair highlighting mirrors word editor patterns.

Generate more

  • Available on existing sentence themes.
  • Uses Pick & Prune.
  • Generated rounds must avoid duplicates with existing kept rounds.
  • Kept rounds append to the theme.

Generation contract

Field Rule Validation note
English prompt Natural English sentence or phrase. Can use normal punctuation. Keep simple by prompt guidance.
Spanish sentence 2 to 8 space-separated words. No commas, semicolons, quotes, parentheses, or slashes.
Distractors Exactly 3 single Spanish words. Unique and not equal to any correct word after normalization.
Theme set No duplicate sentence rounds. Use normalized Spanish sentence as the primary duplicate signal.

Gameplay Rules

Sentence gameplay comes from the Online mock, but production rules must integrate with real duel modes, timers, HP/lives, scoring, and safe client state.

core rules
Mode Sentence behavior Timer Wrong tile Mode tools
PvE One shared sentence board. Players alternate taps. Completion gives shared progress/score. 45 seconds shared. Passes turn. Tracks team mistakes. In HP sessions, costs 1 HP. No PvE hints on sentence rounds in v1.
PvP Each player builds their own copy. Better completion/cleanliness scores better. Exact point values are implementer-owned. 30 seconds per player/round. Tracks player mistakes. In HP sessions, costs 1 HP. No sabotages on sentence rounds in v1.
Relay Picker sends a remaining item. If sentence, answerer builds full sentence alone. 30 seconds. Completion with mistakes scores less than clean completion. In HP sessions, costs 1 HP. Hard-upgrade tokens stay word-only.
Self-duel
(PvE config: challenger === opponent, see lib/duel/selfDuel.ts)		 User builds sentence alone with PvP-style solo board. 30 seconds. Clean completion scores best, completion with mistakes scores less. HP applies only where word HP already applies. Used instead of normal solo practice for sentence themes.

Limited-lives rule

Mirror word HP exactly. If a word wrong choice loses 1 HP, a sentence wrong tile loses 1 HP. If a word timeout loses 1 HP, a sentence timeout loses 1 HP.

Normal non-HP rule

Normal duels do not gain HP just because sentence rounds exist. Use score, timer, and feedback only.

Sentence scoring (matches word scale)

  • Clean completion (no wrong tiles): +2 pts (parity with HARD word).
  • Completion with 1+ wrong tiles: +1 pt (parity with EASY word).
  • Timeout or abandon: 0 pts.
  • PvP: each player scores their own board independently. PvE: team gets the round's points. Relay/self-duel: answerer/user gets the points.
  • No per-tile drip score. The clean-vs-mistakes tier is the entire reward signal.

Implementation Rollout

The safest path is a thin vertical slice, then widening. This feature touches almost everything, so each phase should produce a playable/checkable result.

plan
1

Content foundation

Add theme content type, sentence round validation/normalization, sentence theme create/update/duplicate support, and tests. Keep word-theme behavior intact.

2

Authoring UI

Add Themes filters, sentence cards, new sentence theme generation modal, sentence Pick & Prune, sentence editor, manual add, and generate more.

3

Mixed session layer

Replace word-only session assumptions with mixed session items. Update challenge creation, duel session creation, safe DTOs, and theme selectors.

4

Sentence gameplay engine

Promote the mock sentence engine into production-grade pure logic: repeated words, HP/lives, timers, per-mode state, feedback, and deterministic tests.

5

Duel UI integration

Render sentence rounds in PvP, PvE, Relay, and self-duel. Make tools round-aware. Add final-result handling and round feedback.

6

Weekly goals and limited lives

Allow sentence themes in weekly goals. Both mini-boss and main-boss flows handle mixed word+sentence content (no word-only fallback). Route sentence completion through self-duel/duel-style sessions and mirror word HP/lives behavior.

Risks And Watchpoints

These are the places most likely to create hidden bugs because the old app model is word-first.

watch
High

Word-only assumptions

Many session paths assume every item has word, answer, and wrongAnswers. A discriminated session item type is needed.

High

Relay state machine

Relay currently serves a question snapshot. Sentence items need their own served state, timer, answer resolution, and client-safe hiding.

Medium

HP semantics

Sentence rounds allow multiple wrong taps. Limited-lives sessions must lose 1 HP per wrong tile without accidentally ending normal non-HP duels.

Medium

Generation quality

AI must generate valid short Spanish sentences, not word-card-like entries. Retry validation needs strong errors.

Medium

Mobile tile layout

8 correct words plus 3 distractors means up to 11 tiles. The board must stay readable on small screens.

High

Weekly-goal snapshots

weeklyGoalThemeSnapshots freezes wordType + words[] at goal-lock time. For sentence themes to enter weekly goals (Phase 6), this table needs the same dual-content treatment plus a backfill, and boss-fight content loading must accept mixed items.

Medium

relayLastResult validator

Today relayLastResult.chosen is a single string. A sentence answer is a tile sequence (and possibly a mistake pattern). Either widen chosen to a union or add a parallel relayLastSentenceResult field.

Medium

Per-round tool un-mount

Hint pool and sabotage UIs are mounted session-wide today (footer state is session-level). Switching to per-round mounting touches useHintPool, SabotageRenderer, and the duel footer, and may need round-transition state cleanup.
