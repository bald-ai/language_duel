Highlights

  • Hint model redesigned. The original "gift" hint (one player sends to the other) was replaced by a shared hint pool that affects both players — symmetric, no roles, no consent prompts.
  • Foundational assumption captured. The app now assumes the two players are physically sitting together. That single assumption removed the need for notifications, onboarding, and a lot of UX defensive work.
  • Source of truth is written down. Spec, glossary, and two ADRs all committed. Next session does not need to re-derive any of the 18 decisions.
